Playing Tips

  • This song uses a repeating 4-chord loop throughout — once you nail the progression, you've got the whole song
  • The Key of C version uses open chords and is easier for beginners (no barre chords)
  • For the jazzy feel, let the 7th chord tones ring out — avoid muting strings prematurely
  • Try fingerpicking for verses and switching to light strumming for the chorus to add dynamics
  • The tempo is around 150 BPM but feels relaxed — don't rush the chord changes
  • To match the original key using the easy shapes, place a capo on fret 5 and play the Key of C shapes
